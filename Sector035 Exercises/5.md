## Summary of problem

There is a weird artwork in Indianapolis that [has its own Wikipedia entry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Funky_Bones).

The photo featured on the Wikipedia page can be found all over the internet, but one of the oldest uploads out there is on a certain stock photo site. Are you able to find this stock photo site? The answer is the original filename, including its file extension.
 

## Process

#### Let's get started

Google Lens yields [a link to Getty Images](https://www.gettyimages.ca/detail/news-photo/funky-bones-by-atelier-van-lieshout-2010-commissioned-by-news-photo/590136543?adppopup=true).
 
The file name is listed at the bottom: **2010_in-an-at0552v02.jpg**


## Solution
2010_in-an-at0552v02.jpg
